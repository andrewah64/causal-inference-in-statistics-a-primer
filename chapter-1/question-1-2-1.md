What's wrong with the following?:

Q1) "Data show that income and marriage have a high positive correlation. Therefore improve your earnings by getting married."
A1) There are alternative explanations for why income and marriage are positively correlated. For example, if a person is charming, they may be more likely to be married and have a high income.

Q2) "Data show that as the number of fires increase, so does the number of fire fighters. Therefore, reduce the number of fires by eliminating fire fighters".
A2) We can say that obviously, the number of fire fighters increases in response to the number of fires, not the other way around.

Q3) "People who hurry tend to be late to their meetings. Therefore, don't hurry or be late".
A3) There are alternative explanations for why people are late. For example, Unexpected events extend the duration of events preceding a meeting, which cause the person to hurry to the next meeting to avoid being late.
